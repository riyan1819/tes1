Frequently Asked Questions

Sachi Coin was built for the community, so transparency is at the core of everything we do. Here you’ll find the most common questions about Sachi Coin

What is Sachi Coin?

Sachi Coin is a community-driven memecoin on the Solana blockchain inspired by a real Scottish Fold cat named Sachi. It’s designed to bring joy, positivity, and creativity back to crypto, proving that even the cutest meme can unite people around the world.

How can I Join the Movement?

You can buy SACHI on supported Solana-based decentralized exchanges (DEXs) like Raydium or Jupiter. Simply connect your Solana wallet (such as Phantom or Solflare), search for SACHI , and swap your SOL for SACHI tokens.

Is Sachi Coin safe?

Yes. Sachi Coin is fully community-driven with a transparent foundation, ownership is renounced, liquidity is locked or burnt, and there are no hidden taxes. The project prioritizes safety and trust while delivering fun and positive vibes.

What makes Sachi Coin different from other memecoins?

Unlike most meme projects, Sachi Coin is built around a real story and mission, spreading kindness, joy, and unity across the crypto space. It’s not just about hype; it’s about building a lasting, fun-loving community inspired by a cat with a purpose.
